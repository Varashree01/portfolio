02 / Selected Work

Three projects spanning agentic orchestration, healthcare AI and full-stack product engineering.

Case Study — 01

Multi-Agent AI Orchestration Framework

Supervisor–Worker Agent Architecture

A single AI agent hits a ceiling fast — it can't specialize, and it can't delegate. This framework solves that by coordinating a team of purpose-built agents under one supervisor, each agent owning a narrow task and reporting back through a shared reasoning loop, so complex, multi-step problems get decomposed automatically instead of hand-scripted.

The supervisor routes intent, dispatches work to specialized workers, and reconciles their outputs into one coherent response — backed by a Flask API layer and a React control surface for observing agent behavior in real time.

Case Study — 02

MedTwin AI — Healthcare Digital Twin

AI-Powered Patient Digital Twin

Clinical decisions are safer when they're informed by a living model of the patient, not a static record. MedTwin AI builds a digital twin that mirrors a patient's health data over time, letting clinicians simulate outcomes and surface risks before they become critical.

Retrieval-augmented reasoning grounds every output in real patient context rather than generic inference, turning a stream of health data into an interpretable, continuously updating model of one person.
